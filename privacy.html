dATA cOLLECTION

We use localStorage for session data, member preferences, and encrypted favourites. No advertising cookies are used. No personal data is collected without your explicit action. No third-party tracking scripts or analytics are embedded on this site.

oAUTH aUTHENTICATION

Authentication on [aRTS] is handled via Google Identity Services and Apple Sign In. When you choose to sign in, your device interacts directly with Google or Apple — we receive only a stable, anonymised user identifier (a "sub" claim from the OAuth token), your email address, and your display name. We do not receive or store passwords. Biometric verification (Face ID, Touch ID) is handled entirely by your device's operating system and the OAuth provider — [aRTS] has no access to any biometric data at any point.

dEVICE sESSIONS

When you sign in, a session token is created and stored in your browser's localStorage. This token ties your membership to the current device, allowing you to return to the platform without re-authenticating. Sessions are persistent per device until you explicitly sign out or destroy your ID. If you sign in on multiple devices, each device maintains an independent session. You may revoke device sessions at any time by signing out via your iD dashboard.

iP lOGGING

For security and abuse prevention, your IP address is logged server-side at the moment of each login event. This log entry also records the login provider (Google or Apple), your membership tier, and a timestamp. IP logs are stored on our VPS and are never shared with third parties. Logs are retained for a maximum of 90 days. The IP log does not contain your full OAuth identifier — only a truncated reference. You may request deletion of your IP log entries by contacting us through the TaLK page.

fORMSPREE

Form submissions — including inquiries, reports, and art submissions — are processed through Formspree. Data submitted via forms is stored on Formspree servers and is subject to their privacy policy. [aRTS] does not independently store form submission data.

sTRIPE

Payment processing for premium memberships is handled by Stripe. All payment card details are collected and processed directly by Stripe. [aRTS] does not store, access, or retain payment card information at any point during the transaction.

eNCRYPTION

Favourites data is encrypted using AES-GCM with keys derived from your OAuth identifier. Encrypted data is stored locally in your browser and never leaves your device. [aRTS] has no ability to read, decrypt, or access your favourites data.

tHIRD-pARTY pROVIDERS

By using Google Sign In, you are subject to Google's Privacy Policy. By using Apple Sign In, you are subject to Apple's Privacy Policy. [aRTS] has no control over data collected by these providers during the authentication flow.
